## Overview

> A Lightning Web Component that displays the AI-generated client briefing on the Account record page. Shows portfolio signals, covenant alerts, cross-sell triggers, and meeting prep context in a single, scannable view — proving that the RM gets everything they need without leaving Salesforce.
