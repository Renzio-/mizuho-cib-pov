Customer Problem Statement
Mizuho's Singapore CIB RMs are reactive, not proactive. Client intel is scattered across CRM, portals, compliance systems, and email. Hours are lost weekly on manual meeting prep, call notes, and task tracking. The CRM is a system of obligation ("bankers go in there to capture notes because they have to"), not a system of intelligence. Meanwhile, MUFG has already deployed Salesforce FSC across global CIB — the competitive gap is widening.
Pipeline Context Active Agentforce for Sales opportunity (Mizuho +500 seats, Q4 FY26). This demo aligns to accelerate.
4 Demo Flow: "Morning to Meeting"

Persona: Kenji Nakamura, Senior Coverage RM (Mizuho Singapore)
Value Point: RM's morning starts with AI briefing, walks into meeting prepared, post-meeting gets auto-capture — zero manual data entry.

Journey

1

Agent surfaces today's client briefing with alerts & signals

Prep Deep-Dive

4
Meeting
RM meets client. Informed, prepared, proactive.
5
Post-Meeting
Agent logs summary, assigns tasks, schedules follow-up.

Demo Script

StepActionWhat the Audience Sees
1 RM navigates to Client 360 for "Sumitomo Corporation" Lightning page with briefing panel + Agentforce copilot sidebar
